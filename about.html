About us:

We are a company that develops security software that uses artificial intelligence (AI) to protect businesses and individuals from cyberattacks. Our software is designed to not only identify and block malicious traffic, but also to actively attack against attackers. This is a new and innovative approach to cyber-security, and it has the potential to be very effective.

AI is becoming increasingly sophisticated, and it is being used in a variety of ways to improve security. For example, AI can be used to identify and block malicious traffic, detect malware, and prevent data breaches. LavaShield is taking this a step further by using AI to actively attack against attackers.

When an attacker tries to breach a system protected by LavaShield, our software will identify the attacker and launch a counterattack. This counterattack can disrupt the attacker's operations and make it more difficult for them to launch a successful attack. This can help to protect businesses and individuals from cyberattacks.
